You understand your app intuitively. Your users? Well...

by @lavell.xyz

While building a product, it's easy to get caught up in the illusion that the majority of your users will understand it intuitively. After all, your features and updates are obvious enough, right?

Since launching Hyperspektiv 2 , we've:

  • Received complaints about users losing access to features... that weren't even in the app before the upgrade
  • Gotten 1-stars for removing features... that are still in the app
  • The list goes on...

      • Lesson: Anything critical must be delivered clearly and prominently, else it will be lost on the majority. Don't expect your users to read between the lines.
