From Alienating 👽
To Helpful 🐬

by @lavell.xyz

Imagine you're waiting on the phone for customer service, and:

  • The wait time is 45 minutes
  • Abrasive hold music plays loudly
  • The representative solves your problem, but is rude to you

    • How would you rate this experience on a scale from alienating to helpful?

    👽 ---X-----------------|---------------------- 🐬

    News Flash: Your app is probably just as annoying as this.

    You are presenting a digital interface. It takes careful thought to humanize it. When analyzing your user experience, make sure it's as helpful as possible at each step.
